Cloudiness

The data show that there is no relationship between latitude and cloudiness. However, further anlaysis of the data can help determine a relationship between the cities with very low amounts of clouds and humidity and / or temperature. Also we can notice, that there is more cities with less than 20% of cloudiness and more than 80% cloudiness than the cloudiness between 20%-80%.
